01 · Overview

Privacy at a glance

Blood Sugar Logbook Blutzuckertagebuch ("the App") handles sensitive personal health data. It is built on a fundamental principle: your medical data belongs to you and stays on your device. This policy explains what data the App stores, how it is kept, and what happens when you use the export and import features.

This policy applies to all versions distributed as an Android APK by MiRo Digital Tools, created by Mark Eumir Turingan.

✓ The App does not collect, transmit, remotely store, or share any personal or medical information. All health data remains exclusively on your device.

03 · Storage

How your data is stored

All glucose readings, insulin records, and settings are stored in the App's sandboxed local storage on your device. This data is only accessible to the App and is not shared with other installed apps.

  • Data persists between sessions until you use Reset Data or uninstall the App.
  • Export JSON Backup — writes a .json file silently to Android/data/com.mirodigitaltools.BloodSugarTrackerBlutzuckertagebuch/files/. No share sheet is opened. The file stays in the app folder until you copy or delete it via a file manager.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all local storage and the app data folder. Export a backup first if you want to retain your records.
  • Reset Data permanently wipes all readings, insulin records, and settings and cannot be undone.
Note: MiRo Digital Tools has no access to your device and cannot view, retrieve, or recover any health data stored in the App or exported from it.

05 · Third-Party Services

Third-party services

The App contains no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, social media integrations, or tracking frameworks of any kind.

  • No advertising networks or ad SDKs.
  • No analytics or crash reporting (e.g. Firebase, Crashlytics, Sentry).
  • No social login or identity providers.
  • No cloud storage or synchronisation services.
  • No network requests are made by the App under any circumstances.

All CSV and JSON import processing is performed entirely on your device. The App never transmits imported medical data to any external service.

06 · Medical Disclaimer

Medical disclaimer

Blood Sugar Logbook Blutzuckertagebuch is a personal logging and reference tool. It is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition.

  • The estimated HbA1c displayed in the dashboard is a calculated reference only — always confirm with a laboratory blood test ordered by your doctor.
  • The Insulin Dose Helper is a calculation aid that uses your own doctor-approved values. It is not a prescription. Never adjust your insulin dose solely on the basis of this tool without consulting your medical team.
  • Always follow your doctor's personalised diabetes management plan.
Important: If you are experiencing a medical emergency or your blood sugar readings are severely abnormal, contact emergency services or your healthcare provider immediately. Do not rely on this app in a medical emergency.
07 · Children's Privacy

Children's privacy

The App is intended for adults managing their own health and for caregivers managing a family member's glucose records. It does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Because the App collects no personal information from any user, it presents no special data risk to younger users.

Parental supervision is recommended for any child using the Insulin Dose Helper feature. Always involve a qualified medical professional in a child's diabetes management.
