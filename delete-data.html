Summary

Local-first: Blood sugar readings, notes, settings, and reports are normally stored on your device. MiRo Digital Tools does not operate a cloud account system for this app unless a future version clearly says otherwise.

You can delete app data manually inside the app, by deleting exported files/backups, or by clearing the app storage from Android settings.

This page is provided for users who want a clear data deletion process, especially because the app may contain sensitive health-related logbook information.

What data can be deleted

  • Blood sugar readings and timestamps you entered or imported.
  • Meal tags, notes, remarks, source/device labels, and history records.
  • Dashboard preferences, language/theme settings, report preferences, and local app settings.
  • CSV or JSON backups that you created and saved outside the app.
  • External-support Pro/manual unlock state stored locally on the device, if your version includes manual unlock.
Important: This app is a personal logbook. Deleting data may remove your only copy unless you exported a backup first.

Delete data inside the app

Open Blood Sugar Logbook Tracker

Open the app on the device where your readings are stored.

Review or export first

If you need a copy for your own records or for a healthcare professional, export a CSV/JSON backup or print/save a report before deleting.

Use the app’s delete/reset tools

Delete individual readings from the history/logbook screen, or use any built-in clear/reset option available in your installed version.

Confirm deletion

When prompted, confirm only if you are sure. Deleted local records may not be recoverable unless you kept a backup.

Delete all app data from Android settings

To remove the app’s local storage completely, use Android’s built-in app storage controls. Exact labels may vary by device manufacturer.

Open Android Settings

Go to Settings → Apps → Blood Sugar Logbook Tracker / Blutzuckertagebuch.

Open Storage

Tap Storage or Storage & cache.

Clear storage/data

Tap Clear storage, Clear data, or Delete app data. This removes local readings, settings, and local unlock state stored by the app.

Optional: uninstall the app

Uninstalling the app can also remove app-local data, depending on Android version and backup settings.

Backups and exported files

Deleting app storage does not automatically delete files you exported outside the app, such as CSV reports, JSON backups, PDFs, screenshots, or files shared to cloud storage.

  • Check your Downloads folder, Documents folder, file manager, and cloud apps.
  • Delete shared/exported files manually from each location where you saved or uploaded them.
  • If you emailed or messaged a report to someone, delete it from that service separately if needed.

External-support Pro/manual unlock information

This page is not customized as a Google Play–safe page. If your version includes external-support Pro unlock, donation/support wording, or a manual unlock code, that unlock state is normally stored locally on your device.

Clearing Android app storage or reinstalling the app may remove the local unlock state. Keep any purchase/support confirmation or unlock instructions you received in a safe place.

Contact and deletion requests

If you contact MiRo Digital Tools about data deletion, include the app name and describe what you are trying to delete. Do not send private medical details unless absolutely necessary.

Privacy reminder: Because this app is designed as a local logbook, MiRo Digital Tools may not have any server-side health records to delete. Most deletion is performed on your own device.
